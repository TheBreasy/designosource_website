one woman collective

/wʌn/ - /ˈwʊmən/ - /kəˈlɛktɪv/

The one woman behind the collective is a fool who dreams. But yet has the perseverance to make ideas come to reality. Not afraid to flaunt in the unknown. She acts with courage in her heart,

kindness in her voice and passion in her soul. Firmly believing that every individual is made of an own kind of fairy dust. This motivates her to join forces with creatives with a similar mindset.

One Woman Collective

One woman collective is an ostentatious brand inspired by the complex nature of identities, the fascination with tactility and the force of aesthetics.

It is a paradoxical movement that will ultimately bring soul to your project. For the love of branding, interiors and graphic design.
