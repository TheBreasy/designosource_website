Charities' wishlist

Our local non-profit organisations are performing miracles for our communities, neighborhoods and deprived people. They are all about boosting society in a heartwarming fashion. Together we can return the favor and boost these non-profit organizations.

Charitable institutions can list their most-desired items on our GeSte-platform. Based on those whishlists, visitors can check whether they can meet a demand, and donate a said item so that the non-profit organization can thrive even further.

Together for a circular economy

Not only is this a great way to support charity, it also takes us one step closer to a circular economy. Our consumption-based society marks ‘old’ products as ‘obsolete’ way to quick. We don’t believe in a scrapyard being the final destination of these items when they’re still full of life and use.
